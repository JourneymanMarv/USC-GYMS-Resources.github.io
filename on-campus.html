On-Campus Facilities

Included with Tuition

Strom Thurmond Wellness and Fitness Center

Hours: Mon-Fri 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM; Sat-Sun 9:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Highlights: Weight room, cardio machines, indoor track, swimming pool, rock-climbing wall, group classes, basketball courts

Why Choose Strom: Modern, spacious, and filled with options for all fitness levels

Considerations: Often crowded in the evenings

Included with Tuition

Blatt Physical Education Center

Hours: Mon-Fri: 6–8:30 AM, 11:30 AM–1:30 PM, 4:30–7:00 PM; Sat-Sun 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM

Highlights: Free weights, cardio machines, basketball & volleyball courts, indoor track

Why Choose Blatt: Quieter and less crowded, great for quick workouts

Considerations: Smaller equipment selection compared to Strom
